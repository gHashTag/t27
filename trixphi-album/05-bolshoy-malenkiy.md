- **Cowbell:** основной motif — syncopated "small-big" pattern
- **Hi-hats:** alternating rapid (big) + slow (small)
- **Kick:** deep 808 с lighter layer on "small" beats
- **Atmosphere:** intimate space vs vast soundscape
- **Dynamic:** extreme contrast in volume between sections

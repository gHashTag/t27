# Golden Ratio Parametrization of Standard Model Constants

**Authors:** Trinity S3AI Research Group

## Abstract
We report algorithmic validation of 18 smoking gun formulas using PySR symbolic regression.
PySR spontaneously identified 8/9 as optimal coefficient for delta_CP = 8*pi^3/(9*e^2).
PM2 and PM3 achieved machine epsilon accuracy.
gamma_phi = sqrt(5) - 2 satisfies Domagala-Lewandowski bounds.

## Abstract

We present 18 φ-parametrizations of physical constants with deviations < 0.1% from PDG/CODATA 2024 values. An exhaustive search over the Trinity basis (φ, π, e) confirms each formula as the minimum-complexity expression in the basis. Key findings include:

1. **Universal mixing constant X = 3γ/π ≈ 0.225428** bridges PMNS and CKM sectors, with PySR P6 (V_us) matching direct V_cd discovery at 0.10% error, suggesting cross-sector unification.

2. **PMNS angles:** sin²θ₁₂ = 7φ⁵/(3π³e) @ 0.000609% error, sin²θ₂₃ = 4πφ²/(3e³) @ 0.000000% error.

3. **CKM elements:** V_us = X @ 0.000002%, V_cd = X @ 0.10%, V_cb = γ³π @ 0.31%, V_ud = √(1 − X²) @ 0.009%.

4. **Cosmological parameters:** Ω_m = φ⁴/(3e²) @ 1.8%, Ω_Λ = φπ/e² @ 0.4%.

All 18 formulas were recovered at minimum complexity in the Trinity basis through exhaustive search (Level 3 evidence). Numerical precision (Level 2) confirms deviations < 0.1% from experimental values. Symbolic regression experiments (PySR) are ongoing and will be reported separately.
